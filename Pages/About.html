About Me

IT Specialist & Systems Technician — Sandiacre, Nottinghamshire

Who I Am

I'm Morgan Weekes — a Computer Hardware & Software Technician and Embedded Systems Designer based in Sandiacre, Nottinghamshire. I specialise in building modular systems that integrate hardware precision with software flexibility, solving complex problems with clean, maintainable solutions.

What I Do

  • Diagnostics and troubleshooting across embedded platforms, modular builds, and system-level debugging.
  • Scripting and automation using Batch, Python, and PowerShell to streamline file management and deployment.
  • Interactive system design — Unity input systems, modular state machines, non-blocking animation engines.
  • Media and metadata organisation for large-scale libraries, with an emphasis on consistency and sortable naming.

How I Work

I iterate quickly, document clearly, and avoid boilerplate. Every script I write solves a real problem, and every system I build is designed to scale. I value clarity over cleverness, and I treat technical polish as a requirement, not an optional extra.

What Drives Me

I'm focused on clean workflows, pixel-perfect design corrections, and portfolio-ready documentation. Whether I'm building a smart-home scanner or curating a media archive, I bring order to chaos and ensure the systems I create remain reliable over time.
